# Cohort Program Details

## Program Structure
The OnlyMath Intensive Cohort is a 12-week immersive online program designed to take you from foundational concepts to advanced mathematical mastery.

## Schedule
- **Duration:** 12 Weeks
- **Live Sessions:** Tuesdays and Thursdays, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM (EST)
- **Office Hours:** Saturdays, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (EST)
- **Time Commitment:** 10-15 hours per week (including classes, assignments, and projects)

## What to Expect
- **Live Interactive Lectures:** Deep dives into complex topics with real-time Q&A.
- **Peer Learning:** Weekly study groups and collaborative problem-solving sessions.
- **Continuous Assessment:** Bi-weekly quizzes and a comprehensive final evaluation.
