# Enrollment Process

Joining the OnlyMath Academy cohort is a simple, three-step process:

## Step 1: Application (Online)
Submit your online application. Tell us about your current math background, your goals, and why you want to join the cohort.

## Step 2: Diagnostic Assessment
Take a 45-minute online diagnostic test. This is not a pass/fail exam, but a tool to help us understand your current proficiency in algebra and basic logic so we can place you in the right study group.

## Step 3: Interview (Optional but Recommended)
A brief 15-minute chat with an instructor to align expectations, discuss your goals, and answer any questions you might have about the curriculum.

## Step 4: Registration & Onboarding
Once accepted, you will receive an onboarding package with links to our community Discord, syllabus details, and pre-reading materials.
