# Target Audience

Who is the OnlyMath Academy Cohort for?

## High School Prodigies
Advanced high school students looking to go beyond the standard curriculum and prepare for top-tier university math programs or Olympiads.

## University Students
College students majoring in Computer Science, Engineering, Economics, or Physics who need a stronger mathematical foundation to excel in their core subjects.

## Aspiring Data Scientists & AI Engineers
Professionals pivoting into data-heavy roles who recognize that a deep understanding of Linear Algebra, Calculus, and Statistics is essential for mastering Machine Learning algorithms.

## Lifelong Learners
Anyone with a passion for logic and numbers who wants to appreciate the beauty of mathematics in a structured, supportive environment.
