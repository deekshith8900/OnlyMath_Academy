# Learning Outcomes and Career Paths

## What You Will Learn
By the end of this program, you will have the ability to:
- Read, write, and understand complex mathematical proofs.
- Apply linear algebra concepts to multi-dimensional data.
- Utilize calculus for optimization problems.
- Analyze data sets using probability and statistical models.
- Translate mathematical formulas into computational code (Python).

## Career Opportunities
A strong foundation in math opens doors to the most lucrative and intellectually stimulating careers:
- **Data Scientist / Machine Learning Engineer:** Build AI models powered by linear algebra and calculus.
- **Quantitative Analyst (Quant):** Apply stochastic calculus and statistics to financial markets.
- **Software Engineer (Graphics/Game Engine):** Use vectors and matrices for 3D rendering.
- **Actuary:** Assess risk using advanced probability theory.
