# Bounties and Rewards

We believe in rewarding hard work and mathematical brilliance.

## Top Performer Bounties
- **Valedictorian Award:** The student with the highest overall score at the end of the cohort receives a 50% tuition refund.
- **Best Capstone Project:** A $500 cash bounty for the most innovative and mathematically rigorous final project.

## The "Euler Challenge"
Every week, we post an exceptionally difficult "Euler Challenge" problem. The first student to submit a complete, correct, and elegant proof receives exclusive OnlyMath Academy merchandise and a shoutout in the weekly newsletter.

## Scholarships
We are committed to making math accessible. We offer full and partial scholarships for underrepresented groups in STEM. Apply during the enrollment process.
