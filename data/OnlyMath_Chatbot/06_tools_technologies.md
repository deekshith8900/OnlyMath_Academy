# Tools and Technologies

Modern mathematics relies heavily on computational tools. You will become proficient in the following:

## Visualization & Graphing
- **Desmos:** For interactive 2D graphing and exploring functions.
- **GeoGebra:** For dynamic geometry, algebra, calculus, and 3D mathematics.

## Computational Mathematics
- **Python:** Using libraries such as NumPy, SciPy, and SymPy for numerical and symbolic computation.
- **Jupyter Notebooks:** For creating interactive, reproducible mathematical documents.

## Typesetting
- **LaTeX:** The industry standard for writing professional mathematical formulas and documents. You will learn how to beautifully format all your assignments and proofs.
