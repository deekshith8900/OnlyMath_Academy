# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need prior coding experience?**
A: No prior coding experience is strictly required, but basic familiarity with Python will be helpful for the computational projects. We provide introductory resources for those new to programming.

**Q: What if I miss a live class?**
A: All live sessions are recorded and made available within 24 hours. However, we highly encourage live attendance for interactive Q&A.

**Q: Is there financial aid available?**
A: Yes, we offer need-based scholarships. Please refer to the Bounties & Rewards section or contact our admissions team for more details.

**Q: How difficult is the math?**
A: The program is rigorous. We start from foundational concepts but quickly scale to university-level topics. Commitment and a willingness to struggle through complex problems are required.
